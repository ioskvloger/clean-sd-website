How We Clean

Our Cleaning Process

Every clean follows a structured checklist to ensure nothing is missed. Here's exactly what we do in each room.

01

Pre-Arrival Preparation

  • Inspect property for previous guest items
  • Remove any forgotten belongings
  • Assess cleaning scope and prioritize tasks
  • Prepare supplies and fresh linens
02

Living Areas

  • Reposition furniture to original layout
  • Dust all surfaces and furniture
  • Sanitize light switches and remotes
  • Vacuum and mop all flooring
  • Clean windows and reset TV remotes
03

Bedrooms & Linens

  • Strip and replace all bedding
  • Make beds to hotel standard
  • Dust nightstands and surfaces
  • Clean mirrors and windows
  • Vacuum floors and under beds
04

Bathroom

  • Sanitize sink, toilet, and shower
  • Clean all high-touch surfaces
  • Replace towels and shower mat
  • Refill soap dispensers
  • Empty trash, replace liner
05

Kitchen

  • Clean and sanitize all counters
  • Wash and put away dishes
  • Clean appliances inside and out
  • Restock coffee and essentials
  • Mop floors, empty trash
06

Final Inspection

  • Walk-through every room
  • Photograph completed property
  • Report any damages or issues
  • Confirm Airbnb manual is in place
  • Lock up securely

Our Story

15 Years of Cleaning Excellence

My name is Dmitrii, and I've been perfecting the art of cleaning since 2010, when I worked as an orderly in a hospital intensive care unit. That environment taught me the highest standards of cleanliness, sanitation, and attention to detail — habits that have stayed with me ever since.

After managing restaurants and training staff on surface care, I began working as a professional cleaner in 2016. By 2018, I was running a specialized Airbnb cleaning team in Seoul, South Korea — training cleaners, managing schedules, and overseeing quality across multiple properties simultaneously.

In 2022, I brought that expertise to San Diego. I work with a trusted team of cleaners and have built lasting relationships with local Airbnb hosts who rely on us for every turnover. We're not just a cleaning service — we're a reliable part of your hosting operation.
